Welcome to musico!

musico is an app to manage clients, set lists, and events for the busy musician. It allows you to create a roster of clients, maintain their information and their events all in one centralized location. Your personalized account keeps your data secure!

Not only can you keep track of clients, but you can keep track of your music library and generate set lists at the click of a button.

musico's calendar will help you manage all your events in the app.

To access these features, log in or register an account!
